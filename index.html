Post view

Post view is a small site which allows you to easily share articles from the web. It was made for the FrancesComm discord server, but you can use it to share anywhere.

New View

What?

Post view is a quick way to share articles you find on the web. You can go the New page and fill in the post info, and get a link. This way, you can send only the link, while still being able to share information about the post.
